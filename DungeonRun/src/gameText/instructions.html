Dungeon Run - Instructions

Objective

Your goal is to navigate through the dungeon, defeat enemies, collect key, and survive to reach the final level.

Controls

  • W / A / S / D – UP,LEFT,DOWN,RIGHT
  • shiftTo Run around the map
  • Space - Jump
  • F - Punch
  • E - To launch Inventory attacks
  • R - Pick Up
  • T - Open Chest
  • P - Enable debug mode
  • Y - Switch between Third Person and First Person
  • Q – Switch equipped item
  • M – Open Pause Menu
  • 1 – Punch
  • 2 – Jump Punch
  • 3 – Launch Spell
  • Track Movement Directional To control the camera.

Gameplay Tips

  • Defeat enemies to unlock paths.
  • To Defeat enemies ensure you hit where the health bar is thats how combact is.
  • Keep an eye on your health bar.
  • Time your attacks carefully — enemies can hit back!
  • Open Chests with T and press R to drink potion for health increase!
  • Key only appears when enemies are defeated ,search for it !
  • Collect key after defeating all enemies to advance to the next level by pressing R.
  • Make sure you are close to key ,Press R to pick up

Combact Tips

  • Each Inventory Weapon regenarates after usage.
  • Can switch between the three combat moves.
  • Press 1 to launch Punch and press e to use.
  • Press 2 to launch Jump Punch and press e to use.
  • Press 3 to launch Spirit killer and use e to use.
  • Wait for the attack to reload before using it.

Game Sound

  • Tap anywhere on the screen to allow sound to play
  • Adjust Music volume on the settings page
  • Adjust Click volume on the settings page
  • Browser need user to click anywhere for sound to play

Game Levels

  • Each level has a number of enemies that must be defeated.
  • Move along maps to find hidden chests and hidden enemies.
  • Use inventory attack modes to defend yourself.
  • All enemies are in the game look for them and kill.

Game Camera

  • Move the Camera around to walk in proper direction.
  • Move mouse around to manipulate camera .
  • First person Camera -use the mouse to move according .
  • Third Person Camera -use the track pad to move around .

Game Issues

  • Reload the page to begin at level 1.
  • Pause the game level when stuck and restart level.
  • When fighting enemies ensure you attack directy where their health bar is not their bodies if issues arise.
  • Press P to view debug mode.
  • Jump to move past collisions if stuck spamming space bar-Jump can help.
  • Be careful of Sticky Keys when playing game.
  • To disable sticky fingers press [Ctrl + Alt] or [Ctrl + Alt].
