Dungeon Run - Instructions

Objective

Your goal is to navigate through the dungeon, defeat enemies, collect key, and survive to reach the final level.

Controls

  • W / A / S / D – Move around
  • shiftTo Run around the map
  • Space - Jump
  • F - Punch
  • E - Swing Sword
  • R - Pick Up
  • T - Open Chest
  • G - Push Object
  • X - Death Animation (Auto on death later)
  • E – Fight / Pick up key
  • Q – Switch equipped item
  • 1 / 2 – Select item (Sword or Spell)
  • H – Take damage (for testing)

Gameplay Tips

  • Defeat enemies to unlock paths.
  • Keep an eye on your health bar.
  • Time your attacks carefully — enemies can hit back!
  • Collect key after defeating all enemies to advance to the next level.
