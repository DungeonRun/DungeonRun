Dungeon Run - Instructions

Objective

Your goal is to navigate through the dungeon, defeat enemies, collect key, and survive to reach the final level.

Controls

  • W / A / S / D – Move around
  • shiftTo Run around the map
  • Space - Jump
  • F - Punch
  • E - To launch Inventory attacks
  • R - Pick Up
  • T - Open Chest
  • G - Push Object
  • Q – Switch equipped item
  • M – Open Pause Menu
  • 1 – Punch
  • 2 – Jump Punch
  • 3 – Launch Spirit killer

Gameplay Tips

  • Defeat enemies to unlock paths.
  • To Defeat enemies ensure you hit where the health bar is thats how combact is.
  • Keep an eye on your health bar.
  • Time your attacks carefully — enemies can hit back!
  • Open Chests with T and press R to drink postion for health increase!
  • Key only appears when enemies are defeated ,search for it !
  • Collect key after defeating all enemies to advance to the next level by pressing R.
  • Make sure you are close to key ,Press R to pick up

Combact Tips

  • Press 1 to launch Punch and press e to use.
  • Press 2 to launch Jump Punch and press e to use.
  • Press 3 to launch Spirit killer and use e to use.
  • Wait for the attack to reload before using it.

Game Sound

  • Tap anywhere on the screen to allow sound to play
  • Adjust Music volume on the settings page
  • Adjust Click volume on the settings page
  • Browser need user to click anywhere for sound to play

Game Issues

  • Reload the page
  • Jump to move past collisions if stuck
